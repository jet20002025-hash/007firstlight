007 First Light Casts Patrick Gibson as Young James Bond

IO Interactive reveals the actor behind the youngest on-screen 007 in gaming history. The Irish actor brings fresh energy to the iconic spy role in this origin story adventure.

DEVELOPMENT Aug 22, 2025

The Creative Pitch That Secured the Bond License

IO Interactive reveals the ingenious demonstration that convinced MGM and Eon to grant them the James Bond video game rights using Hitman's signature social stealth gameplay.

The Daniel Craig Demo: IO Interactive CEO Hakan Abrak revealed that the studio created a unique pitch by modeling Daniel Craig's head and placing it on Agent 47's body in the beloved Sapienza map from Hitman: World of Assassination. This creative demonstration showcased their vision for Bond's social stealth gameplay.

Social Stealth Vision: Rather than focusing solely on action and shooting, IO wanted to demonstrate Bond's ability to navigate social spaces using charm, bluff, and wit. The Sapienza map, with its sun-soaked Italian streets and complex social dynamics, proved perfect for showcasing this approach to the license holders.

Origin Story Focus: The studio emphasized their commitment to creating an authentic Bond experience from the ground up, exploring the untold story of a young James learning the ropes of espionage rather than starting as the fully-formed 007.
